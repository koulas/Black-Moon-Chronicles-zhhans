Skirmish

When you choose the Skirmish option in the main menu, you compose your army, and you fight an enemy regiment on a map that you have created in the level editor. However, you can place additional units directly on the map, in the editor.

 

Region Map

  • IMPORTANT Regiments and unique places
    Unique places exist that are particularly well-defended so that a single regiment is not sufficient to win. It is therefore possible to bring in several regiments. It is necessary merely for the regiments of the player to be adjacent to the unique place. If one regiment goes in, then all of the adjacent regiments take part in the battle.
  • Composing a regiment
    You can see the units making up a regiment by selecting it or by rolling over it with the mouse.

 

Battle Map

  • Particularities of the units
    Archers are more effective from on high (mountains) and from ramparts, these two bonuses being cumulative.
    Archers double the damage inflicted on airborne creatures.
    Cavalry increase damage against infantry and archers.
    Phalanxes increase damage against cavalry.
    All the magicians and priests are immunized against fire except those of the Black Moon.
  • Short-code keys
    If there is an ally castle on a battle map, you can open the gate by pressing the "Enter" key.
