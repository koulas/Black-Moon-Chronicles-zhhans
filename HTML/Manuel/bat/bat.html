EMPIRE

Buildings available from the outset of the game :

  • Castle
  • Joinery
  • Delegation
  • Barracks

 

The Campsite
The Campsite allows you to raise, train and accomoodate your troops. Through the Campsite, your buy and organize your army before going into battle on the region map.

There are three possible ways to improve your Campsite :

 

The Barracks

  • Enables all of the troops available to the player to be bought
  • Enables Level 1 Soldiers to be produced

 

The Casern

  • Enables all of the troops available to the player to be bought
  • Enables Level 2 Soldiers to be produced

 

The Quarters

  • Enables all of the troops available to the player to be produced
  • Enables Level 3 Soldiers to be produced

 

 

The Embassy
The Embassy is the place where you exercise diplomacy over the different sides: Empire, Black Moon, Justice, Light, Elves and Dwarves. The Embassy is also the place where you have access to the region maps where you can fight. Finally, the Embassy allows you to carry out research to improve the resale of your units.

There are three possible ways of improving the Embassy :

 

The Delegation

  • Allows research into Level 1 Trade

 

The Consulate

  • Allows research into Level 2 Trade

 

The Embassy

  • Allows research into Level 3 Trade

 

 

The Sawmill

The sawmill is your contruction building par excellence. The sawmill also allows you to produce better quality bows, as well as certain siege engines.

There are three possible ways to improve the Sawmill :

 

The Joinery

  • For building the Stable
  • For building the Forge
  • For producing the Level 1 Archer
  • For researching and producing the Level 1 Catapult
  • For researching Level 1 Wood Science
  • For researching and producing Level 1 Battering Ram
  • For researching the Distance Bow

 

The Carpentry

  • For building the Stud Farm
  • For building the Furnaces
  • For building the Casern
  • For producing the Level 2 Archer
  • For researching and producing the Level 2 Catapult
  • For researching Level 2 Wood Science
  • For researching the Power Bow

 

The Cabinet-Maker's

  • For building the Foundry
  • For building the Quarters
  • For producing the Level 3 Archer
  • For researching and producing the Level 3 Catapult
  • For researching Level 3 Wood Science
  • For researching the Precision Bow

 

 

The Forge

The forge is the workshop where weapons, shields and armour are manufactured. The forge is absolutely necessary for the construction of certain buildings.

There are three possible ways to improve the Forge :

 

The Forge

  • For building the Drill Room
  • For building the Chapel
  • For building the Magicians' House
  • For researching Level 1 Weapons
  • For researching Level 1 Armour

 

The Furnaces

  • For building the Arms Room
  • For building the Church
  • For building the Magicians' School
  • For researching Level 2 Weapons
  • For researching Level 2 Armour
  • For researching and producing Burning Projectiles
  • For researching and producing the Level 1 Balista

 

The Foundry

  • For building the Magicians' Academy
  • For researching Level 3 Weapons
  • For researching Level 3 Armour

 

 

The Stable
The stable provides for raising, training and lodging cavalrymen. The stable also makes it possible to conduct researche into improving the cavalry units.

There are two possible ways to improve the Stable :

 

The Stable

  • For producing Level 1 Light Cavalrymen
  • For researching Speed Cavalry

 

The Stud Farm

  • For producing Level 2 Light Cavalrymen
  • For researching the Cavalry Charge
  • For producing the Level 1 Imperial Leviathan

 

 

The Armory

The Armory provides for raising, training and lodging knights and paladins. The Armory also makes it possible to study new combat techniques which make your Knights and paladins even more fearsome.

There are two possible ways to improve the Armory :

 

The Drill Room

  • For producing Level 1 Knights
  • For researching Level 1 Vorpale Attack

 

The Armory

  • For producing Level 2 Knights
  • For researching Level 2 Vorpale Attack
  • For researching Knight's Fury

 

 

The Magicians' Tower

The Magicians' Tower provides for raising, training and lodging Magicians. The Magicians' Tower provides for learning the Magicians' spells.

There are three possible ways to improve the Magicians' Tower :

 

The Magicians' House

  • For producing Level 1 Magicians
  • For researching Level 1 Mana Recovery
  • For using the MG 0 spell
  • For researching the MG 4 spell
  • For producing the Level 1 Imperial Dragon

 

The Magicians' School

  • For producing Level 2 Magicians
  • For researching Level 2 Mana Recovery
  • For researching the MG 2 and MG 3 spells
  • For producing the Level 2 Imperial Dragon
  • For researching Level 1 Mana Power
  • For researching Level 1 Mana Quantity

 

The Magicians' Academy

  • For producing Level 3 Magicians
  • For researching Level 3 Mana Recovery
  • For researching the MG 1 and MG 5 spells
  • For producing the Level 3 Imperial Dragon
  • For researching Level 2 Mana Power
  • For researching Level 2 Mana Quantity

 

 

The Place of worship
