Here is a region dominated by the Empire. Large cities like Feyhin Lokcthat or Magistrya dominate the region. Feyhin Lokcthat, the city of gambling, will perhaps enable you to make your fortune. Magistrya, the city of the Magicians, is governed by a powerful mage whom it is preferable to have as a friend... To the north-east lie numerous forests inhabited by Elves. Legends also tell of a long-forgotten great peak where the winds live.//
