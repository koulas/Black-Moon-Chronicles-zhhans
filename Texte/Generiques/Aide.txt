                      - 帮助 (战斗区域) -


Space : 切换战斗/移动模式

1-9 : Cast a spell

A : Select all the units
C : Center the view on the current selection
W : Center the view on Wismerhill, then on the heroes
S : Stop/Re-start time

Enter : Open/Close the gate of the castle

Ctrl+0-9 : Store a combat group
Alt+0-9  : Recall a stored combat group

Tab : Show/Hide the mini-map
B : Access to the bestiary

Left button  on mouse : Selections
Right button on mouse : Orders/Actions
Multi-selection : Shift or Ctrl + Left click

Moving over the map : Arrows or Mouse pointer on the edges of the screen

N : Show/Hide clouds
L : Lighting Yes/No
D : Show/Hide damage
M : Show/Hide messages

Escape: Interface/Quit//
