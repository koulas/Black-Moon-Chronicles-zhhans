The faults and the sins of Ghorghor Bey have been numerous and terrible... In order for his soul to return to this world, a substantial donation will have to be given to the clergy!  To buy back his sad soul, you need 1,000 gold crowns, but, alas, you do not have such a sum!  No priest will risk bringing Ghorghor Bey back to life for less gold than that. He is therefore for ever condemned to err in the limbo of the dead...//
